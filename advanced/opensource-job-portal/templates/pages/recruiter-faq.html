FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

RECRUITER :

Q : How can i login to my account?
A: Please go to the homepage of peeljobs.com and select the recruiter menu to find login. Enter your registered email id and password, then click Login to login into your account.

Q : How can i post a job requirement ?
A : If you are a consultancy or any job agency then you have to register your consultancy name in our company. Then you can post your jobs from there, no need to contact every time.

Q : How can i search for resumes(profiles) at peeljobs.com ?
A : Resume with title are available in our site. you can directly search for title it will show required profiles.

Q : How can I see/view the application received for my job post ??
A : After login,please go to home page, there you can see job list. In every job list there is a link Applied Jobs. It will show all the candidate list applied to job.

Q : How can i edit job details ?
A : Please go to home page, there you can find a link edit this job. You can edit that job there only.

A : You can provide your email id in reset password. one auto generated password will be send to you, using this password you can login again and change your password as you want.

Q : How can i register in peeljobs.com?
A : You can register online yourself along with your company details.

Q : How will i know my application/job post is unsuccessful ??
A : you will get a message that job is not posted successfully, later you can post your job again without any problems.

Q : On what basis do you make your selection?
A : Candidates are screened and shortlisted on the basis of job requirements, such as level of qualifications and experience related to position they are applying for.

Q : What happens to the Resume posted?
A : After posting the Resume, it will go to the Resume database of Peeljobs Job Portal where the team will review the resume and consider it for suitable jobs. The resumes will be given to the registered Employers who have been verified and approved by the Administrator of Company.These Employers will contact the suitable candidates.
